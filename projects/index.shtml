Stuff we're up to these days...

SlothWerks continues its tradition as a partner to the community, tackling a variety of multi-media, web development, and special event coordination projects for area not-for-profits. As with most things, SlothWerks approaches each project with an eye for detail and the delicate care that every client deserves.

Current Projects

  1. Challenge Walk MS event videos for the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Wisconsin Chapter
  2. Web management for Team N3kk1d and Mulick Park Elementary (on-going)
  3. A web-based local business recommendation database for the Eastgate Neighborhood Association (on-going)

We hope to add additional projects to this list as we delve back into the world of web development via Grand Circus, a community-centric technical training group operating out of Detroit and Grand Rapids, Michigan.

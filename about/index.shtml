Just what is SlothWerks?

SlothWerks is the largely not-for-profit face of Sloth, a multi-media producer and audio/video engineer based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. While the SlothWerks commercial website is designed to entice new business to the Underground Production Studio, the focus of this portfolio is on web design. We heartily thank Grand Circus for this opportunity to once again delve into "pure code" and, hopefully, a new chapter in the professional development of the SlothWerks brand.

In the past, Sloth has developed web projects for a wide variety of clients, including WYCE Radio, the Smith College Smithereens, the Grand Rapids Peninsular Club, City High / Middle School PTSA, and Mulick Park Elementary. For a taste of what we're up to these days, visit our projects page.

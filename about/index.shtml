Just what is SlothWerks?

Do. Or do not. There is no try.
- Yoda

SlothWerks is the largely not-for-profit face of Sloth, a multi-media producer and audio/video engineer based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. We heartily thank Grand Circus for this opportunity to once again delve into "pure code" and, hopefully, a new chapter in the professional development of the SlothWerks brand. To learn more about SlothWerks' commercial venture, visit SlothWerks.Com.

In the past, Sloth has developed web projects for a wide variety of clients, including WYCE Radio, the Smith College Smithereens, the Grand Rapids Peninsular Club, City High / Middle School PTSA, and Mulick Park Elementary. For a taste of what we're up to these days, visit our projects page.

While not engaged in the tech sector, Sloth enjoys music, tabletop gaming, cycling, and wondering why he spent so much money purchasing the Nintendo Switch.
